# Notes From a Quiet Launch

Today the site becomes a home for working notes, finished essays, and ideas that are still gathering shape.

## What changed

- Markdown files now render as article pages
- The archive groups posts by year
- Search checks titles, tags, and descriptions
- Tags can narrow the list without leaving the page

## A publishing rhythm

Draft locally, keep the prose simple, and let each post earn its place. When a draft is ready, add it to `posts.json` and publish.

> A good archive is a promise to future readers and future you.

The first version does not need much ceremony. It needs a dependable path from thought to page.
